What do we offer?

Hatha

A slower, beginner-friendly style focused on basic poses and breathing.
Good for: beginners, relaxation, learning proper technique
What to expect: simple poses, pauses between movements, calm pace
Result: better posture, flexibility, less stress

Vinyasa

A dynamic, flowing style where movements are connected with breath.
Good for: people who want something active but not extreme
What to expect: continuous movement, smooth transitions, light cardio
Result: improved flexibility, strength, and endurance

Yin

A very slow, deep style where poses are held for several minutes.
Good for: stretching, recovery, stress relief
What to expect: long holds, minimal movement, deep relaxation
Result: increased flexibility, joint mobility, mental calm

Power

A more intense, fitness-focused version of yoga.
Good for: people who want a workout
What to expect: fast pace, strength exercises, sweating
Result: muscle strength, stamina, calorie burn

Ashtanga

A structured, traditional style with a fixed sequence of poses.
Good for: disciplined people who like routine
What to expect: same sequence every class, physically demanding
Result: strength, flexibility, strong body control

Hot

Yoga practiced in a heated room.
Good for: people who like intense sweating
What to expect: high temperature, challenging sessions
Result: flexibility, detox-like feeling (mostly sweat), endurance
